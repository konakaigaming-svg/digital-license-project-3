Project 3 Presentation

The "Forbidden Fruit" Paradox

Total age bans create a dangerous Digital Vacuum. When technology is prohibited rather than explained, students bypass restrictions through VPNs and anonymous accounts.

Much like the dystopian society in Fahrenheit 451, where banning books led to a loss of critical thinking, banning digital platforms suppresses the skills needed to be an informed citizen.

Strategic Insight

"Resilience is built through practice, not avoidance."

Research Summary

Impact on Regulation

McAlister (2024): Bans limit emotional regulation training, increasing vulnerability upon eventual access.

Social Vulnerability

WHO (2021): Social media is a vital lifeline. Bans sever support networks for marginalized youth.

The Resilience Model

Vissenberg (2022): "Guided Exposure" is the only proven method to build recovery skills.

Effectiveness Analysis

72% of teens circumvent digital bans. Prohibition is "security theater" that does not stop usage.

Community & Support Systems

Digital platforms create critical social connection and safety resources for youth.

Curriculum Framework: The 3 Pillars

Privacy, data permanence, tracking, and auditing digital footprints.

Algorithmic IQ

Deconstructing dopamine loops and recognized predatory patterns.

Emotional Health

Managing conflict, "Filtered Reality," and digital mindfulness.

Shift district resources from Restrictive Monitoring to Standardized Literacy Training.
